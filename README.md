## Objective 🥅

Avoid the obstacles 🪨, and survive 🔥. Try to beat your high score 💯!

## How to Play 🤔

- Press Space Bar to start 🔘
- Use Left and Right arrow keys to move left and right ↔️
- Avoid the obstacles 🪨
